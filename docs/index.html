AeroLex – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 April 2026

AeroLex ("the App") is operated by Rhino Consulting (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the App.

1. Information We Collect

AeroLex is designed to operate offline with minimal data collection.

  • No account registration required. You do not need to create an account to use the App.
  • Subscription data. If you purchase a subscription, Google Play processes the transaction. We receive only a subscription status token — we do not receive your name, email address, or payment details.
  • AI query count. The number of AI queries you have used this month is stored locally on your device only (via Android DataStore). This data never leaves your device.
  • Ask AI feature. When you use the Ask AI feature, your question and relevant regulation excerpts are sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API. We do not log, store, or process these queries on our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking libraries.
  • We do not have a backend server — the App communicates only with Google Play (billing) and Google Gemini API (AI queries, when used).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing – handles all subscription payments. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini API – processes Ask AI queries when you use that feature. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy above.

4. Data Storage

All regulation data is stored locally on your device and is included in the App package. No regulation data is transmitted to any server. Your T&C acceptance and AI query count are stored locally using Android DataStore and are not synced anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy

AeroLex is intended for aviation professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. POPIA Compliance (South Africa)

In terms of the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA), we process no personal information of users. Subscription status is processed by Google Play under their own compliance obligations. If you have any privacy concerns, contact us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
